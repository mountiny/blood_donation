    Donating blood is easy!
    1
    Check if you are eligible to donate blood. We go you covered - click here!
    2
    Book an appointment with a donation centre close by. We have a map for registered users!
    3
    Before donation, drink plenty of water, eat regular meals and avoid vigurous exercise.
    4
    Once you arrive, a nurse will do a quick health screening to make sure you are safe to donate blood.
    5
    You will be seated and a nurse will begin the procedure which should take between 5 and 10 minutes.
    6
    After the donation, you are encouraged to take a moment to relax, drink plenty and you can go home.

    Can I donate? Yes, if:
    1
    You are fit and healthy?
    You had no infection in last 2 weeks.
    2
    You weight between 50 and 158 kilograms.
    You are between 17 and 70 years old.
    3
    No holiday outside UK in last 4 months.
    No dental treatment in last 7 days.
    4
    If you have been pregnant, you cannot give blood until 6 months after giving a birth.
    You have not got any piercing or tattoo in last 4 months.
